Welcome To ShuleEazy
Making school fun and accessible to everyone

ShuleEazy is an online school offering the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) for students and free Mentorship session for teachers, parents and students

Why ShuleEazy Making a difference in Education
Personalized Attention
Our classes are designed for personalized attention for all our learners. Our small class sizes (10-15 students) enable group dynamic and peer interaction & give room for personalized needs. Teachers can easily interact with the students and help them in their areas of weakness.
Quality & Affordable Education
We deliver a child-centered, interest and skill-based curriculum that is focused on the holistic development of the individual children. We make education not only fun but also accessible to everyone. The classes are affordable and one can be mentored by experts in the industry for free!
Quality Teachers
Our accredited and vetted teachers are driven to help our students flourish and thrive. They inspire, instill confidence, encourage, know, and believe in their students. They are the best in what they do and are highly trained in the Competency Based Curriculum(CBC)
Quality Members
We have professionals in different fields who volunteer to mentor students, teachers and parents in their areas of expertise. These are professionals who want to turn their passions into conversations, friendships and networks. They help those interested in their field understand it and start off.
Progress Tracking
Our platform allows Transparent Daily Progress Tracking for parents: with daily diary entries, Assignment & Attendance tracking and merits. Parents can check the performance of their children, the assignments given and also whether they are attending the classes, all at the comfort of their homes.
Amazing Community
We offer a platform where the learners, teachers, parents and mentors can all interact, share different ideas and do various projects together. This is done using slack, where we have socialization beyond classroom learning. A group can agree among themselves either to meet virtually or physically.
